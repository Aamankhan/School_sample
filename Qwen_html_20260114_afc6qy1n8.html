About Ekta Public School

Ekta Public School was founded in 2005 with a vision to provide quality education that nurtures the intellectual, physical, emotional, and social development of every child. Located in the heart of the city, our school has grown to become one of the most respected educational institutions in the region.

Our philosophy is centered around the belief that every child is unique and deserves an education that helps them discover their potential and develop into confident, compassionate, and responsible citizens.

Our Mission & Vision

Vision: To be a premier educational institution that empowers students to become lifelong learners and responsible global citizens.

Mission: To provide a stimulating and supportive learning environment that fosters academic excellence, character development, creativity, and critical thinking.

Admissions Process

Welcome to Ekta Public School! We are delighted that you are considering our school for your child's education. Our admissions process is designed to be simple and transparent.

Admission Guidelines

Admissions are open for classes Nursery to Grade XII. The academic year begins in April. We admit students throughout the year based on seat availability.

Age Criteria: As per CBSE guidelines

Admission Steps

Registration

Complete the online registration form and pay the registration fee.

Interaction Session

Attend an interaction session with the school counselor (for parents) and assessment (for students).

Document Submission

Submit required documents including birth certificate, previous school records, and photographs.

Fee Payment

Pay the admission and annual fees to confirm your child's seat.

Required Documents

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school leaving certificate (for Grade I and above)
  • Report card of the previous class
  • Four passport-sized photographs of the child
  • Two passport-sized photographs of parents
  • Aadhaar card of child and parents
